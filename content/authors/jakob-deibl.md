Jakob Deibl is Tenure-Track-Professor at the Faculty for Catholic Theology and scientific manager of the Research Centre for Religion and Transformation at the University of Vienna. He studied Catholic Theology and Religious Education in Salzburg and Vienna. From 2008 to 2011 he worked as a research associate (prae doc) at the Department of Fundamental Theology at the Faculty of Catholic Theology at the University of Vienna. His doctoral dissertation was on Gianni Vattimo. It was published with an introduction written by Vattimo himself as the fifth volume of the series “Religion and Transformation in Contemporary European Society”. In 2012.
