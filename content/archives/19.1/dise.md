Now the life of God is the death of God, for resurrection is crucifixion, just as crucifixion is resurrection . . . resurrection is apocalypse, and is the apocalypse of the eternal act of God, an apocalypse which is the apocalypse of God, but nevertheless and even thereby the apocalypse of act or actuality, and thus an apocalypse which is all in all."
